Mythological Stuff

by Jack Huo

A site about four cool Chinese mythological creatures

Github Link: A01

This site is about a motley collection of some chinese mythological creatures that I find interesting and their chinese characters. There are many, many, many of these creatures in the Chinese mythos. Commonly, they are categorized into groups of four, with each grouping having a common theme and each animal representing something. For example: The Four Heavenly Beasts, The Four Auspicious Beasts, The Four Symbols, etc. This page will go over another group of four:
麒麟 Qilin
狻猊 Suanni
貔貅 Pixiu
饕餮 Taotie
Why did I choose these four? Because the I think the Chinese characters for their names are cool. This site will examine the components of these characters. Otherwise, I don't believe these creatures have much in common.
